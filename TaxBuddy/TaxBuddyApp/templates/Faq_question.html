Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about income tax and filing in Pakistan.

Individuals earning taxable income above the minimum threshold are required to file income tax returns with FBR.
ATL is a list published by FBR containing names of individuals who have filed their income tax returns.
A filer submits annual tax returns and enjoys lower tax rates, while a non-filer pays higher withholding taxes.
You can use our Salary, Business, or AOP tax calculators available above to compute your estimated tax liability.
