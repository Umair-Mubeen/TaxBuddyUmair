Your trusted educational platform for understanding Pakistan's tax laws, compliance rules, and FBR regulations.

AdSense Banner (728x90) AdSense (300x250)

Our Mission

TaxBuddy Umair is a Pakistan-based educational platform focused on simplifying income tax, sales tax, and compliance matters under the Income Tax Ordinance 2001 and relevant FBR regulations.

The core goal of this platform is to help salaried individuals, freelancers, business owners, and Associations of Persons (AOP) understand complex tax laws in a clear, practical, and accessible manner. We believe that an informed taxpayer is an empowered taxpayer.

What We Offer

Whether you are looking for general tax information or need specific tools to estimate your liabilities, our platform provides a comprehensive suite of free, educational resources.

Detailed Tax Guides

Easy-to-understand compliance tutorials and rules for income, sales, and property taxes.

Smart Calculators

Updated tools for calculating salary, business, and AOP tax based on the latest FBR slabs.

Filer & ATL Status

Practical explanations on Active Taxpayer benefits, withholding tax, and maintaining compliance.

Business Support

Educational resources specifically tailored for individuals, freelancers, and small businesses.
