How to Check ATL Status

1
Visit FBR ATL Portal
Go to atl.fbr.gov.pk — FBR's official Active Taxpayers List portal
2
Select Search Type
Choose from: CNIC (for individuals), NTN (for businesses/companies), or Registration No.
3
Enter Your Number
Enter your 13-digit CNIC (without dashes) or NTN number and click Search
4
Check Result
If your name appears — you are an Active Filer ✅. If not — you are a Non-Filer ❌

📱 Check ATL via SMS

Send SMS to 9966:

ATL <space> CNIC
Example: ATL 3520112345671

📝 How to Become a Filer

  1. Register on IRIS portal — iris.fbr.gov.pk
  2. Obtain your NTN (National Tax Number)
  3. File your income tax return for the latest tax year
  4. Your name appears on ATL within 2-3 days of filing
Return filing deadline: 30 September (Section 118, ITO 2001)

⚠️ Late Filer ATL Surcharge

If you file your return after the due date (30 September), you can still appear on ATL as a Late Filer by paying an ATL surcharge of Rs. 1,000. Late filers pay intermediate rates — higher than active filers but lower than non-filers.
