How to Check ATL Status

1
Visit FBR IRIS Portal
Go to iris.fbr.gov.pk — FBR's official IRIS portal
2
Click "Online Verifications"
On the IRIS homepage scroll down to Online Verifications section — no login required
3
Click "Active Taxpayer List (Income Tax)"
From the left panel select Active Taxpayer List (Income Tax) — the verification form will open on the right
4
Select Parameter Type
Select from dropdown: NTN, CNIC, Passport No., or Reg/Inc. No. — then enter your Registration No.
5
Enter Captcha & Click Verify
Enter the Captcha code shown on screen and click Verify. Result will show immediately — Active Filer ✅ or Non-Filer ❌

📱 Also Check via ATL Portal (Alternative)

You can also check directly at FBR's ATL portal:

atl.fbr.gov.pk/atl/online/
Enter CNIC or NTN directly — no captcha required on this portal

❌

📝 How to Become a Filer

  1. Register on IRIS portal — iris.fbr.gov.pk
  2. Obtain your NTN (National Tax Number)
  3. File your income tax return for the latest tax year
  4. Your name appears on ATL within 2-3 days of filing
Return filing deadline: 30 September (Section 118, ITO 2001)

⚠️ Late Filer ATL Surcharge

If you file your return after the due date (30 September), you can still appear on ATL as a Late Filer by paying an ATL surcharge of Rs. 1,000. Late filers pay intermediate rates — higher than active filers but lower than non-filers.
