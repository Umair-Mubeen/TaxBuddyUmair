Taxes on Immovable Property

In Pakistan, taxes on property include CGT, Stamp Duty, and Withholding Tax. Sales Tax and Withholding Tax apply to transactions. CGT rates depend on holding periods.

Salaried Individuals and Business Individuals

In Pakistan, salary income is taxed progressively, with deductions and credits available. Business income is taxed at a flat rate for companies, while sole proprietors and partnerships follow individual income tax slabs. Both are subject to Withholding Tax and Sales Tax on certain transactions.
