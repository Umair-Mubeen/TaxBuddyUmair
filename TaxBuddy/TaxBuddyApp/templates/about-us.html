Pakistan Tax Educator & Content Creator · Karachi

Tax Educator Content Creator

I am Umair Mubeen — a Pakistan tax educator and content creator based in Karachi. I started TaxBuddy Umair to bridge the gap between complex tax laws and everyday Pakistanis who struggle to understand their tax obligations. I run this platform independently, creating free educational content on income tax, sales tax, and FBR compliance.

🎯 My Mission

To make Pakistan's tax system understandable and accessible for every citizen — salaried individuals, business owners, freelancers, and students. I believe that tax literacy is a fundamental right, not a privilege reserved for those who can afford a tax consultant.
