Allowable deductions under Section 15A, ITO 2001

  • One-fifth of gross rent as repairs allowance
  • Fire, theft and other insurance premiums
  • Local rates and taxes on the property
  • Ground rent payable in respect of the property
  • Interest/profit on capital borrowed to purchase property
  • HBFC or scheduled bank repayments
  • Administration and collection charges
  • Legal charges for rent recovery
  • Irrecoverable rent previously assessed
