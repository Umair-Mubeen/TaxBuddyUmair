Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

TaxBuddy Umair ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website www.taxbuddyumair.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact form submissions: Name, phone number, email address, and message content when you fill our contact form.
  • Comments: Name, email, and comment text when you post a comment on a blog article.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, and device type — collected automatically via Google Analytics.
  • Cookies: Small text files placed on your device by us or third-party services (see section 5).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your contact form enquiries or WhatsApp consultations
  • To display and moderate blog comments
  • To analyse website traffic and improve content (via Google Analytics)
  • To display relevant advertisements (via Google AdSense)

4. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this website. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymised data about website usage. This data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy. IP anonymisation is enabled on this website.

6. Cookies

This website uses the following types of cookies: Essential cookies required for the website to function; Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to measure traffic; and Advertising cookies (Google AdSense) to display relevant ads. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Contact Form Data

Information submitted via our contact form is stored in our database solely for the purpose of responding to your query. We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is retained for a maximum of 2 years.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
