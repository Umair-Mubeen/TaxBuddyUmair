Understanding Filer vs. Non-Filer Status

Filing your tax return via the FBR portal is crucial. A Filer appears on the Active Taxpayer List (ATL) after submitting an annual return and benefits from heavily reduced tax rates. A Non-Filer does not submit returns and is subject to significantly higher withholding taxes on property, vehicles, banking, and other transactions.

Tax Compliance Deadlines

The standard income tax filing deadline in Pakistan is 30th September following the end of the tax year. Non-filing may result in penalties, higher withholding tax deductions, restrictions on financial transactions, and possible legal action from the Federal Board of Revenue (FBR).

Q1 • Income Tax ITO, 2001

The paid-up capital plus undistributed reserves of a Small Company must not exceed:

A. Rs. 100 million
B. Rs. 75 million
C. Rs. 50 million
D. Rs. 250 million
Attempt Full Test
Explanation:

Under Section 2(59AB)(i), a Small Company must have paid-up capital plus undistributed reserves not exceeding Rs. 50 million.

Section 2(59AB). Small Company - ITO, 2001
Q2 • Income Tax ITO, 2001

Which section of the Income Tax Ordinance, 2001 deals with Income from Business?

A. Section 37
B. Section 39
C. Section 18
D. Section 12
Attempt Full Test
Explanation:

Any income derived from business, other than exempt income, is chargeable to tax under the head Income from Business.

Section 18. Income from Business - ITO, 2001

Frequently Asked Questions

The Active Taxpayer List (ATL) is published by FBR and includes names of taxpayers who have filed their income tax returns. Individuals listed on ATL enjoy lower withholding tax rates on various transactions.

A filer appears on the ATL after submitting an annual return and benefits from reduced tax rates. A non-filer does not submit returns and is subject to higher withholding taxes on property, vehicles, banking, and other transactions.

Income tax is calculated according to the applicable tax slabs for salaried individuals, business income, or AOP. You can use our updated tax calculators to estimate your liability based on current rates.
